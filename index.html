ඒකාකීය ස්වභාවය

Loneliness does not announce itself. It builds in the spaces between unanswered messages.

◈

බිය

Fear of being seen. Fear of not being seen. Both can silence someone completely.

◇

ගෙරවීම

Bullying leaves no visible scars — only echoes that replay in the mind at 3am.

○

තේරුම් ගැනීම

Empathy is not a feeling. It is a choice. Ask someone how they are — and wait for the real answer.

●

ශ්‍රවණය

To truly hear someone is to give them the rarest gift — the feeling that they exist.

◆

නිහඬකම

Silence is not peace. Sometimes silence is a scream no one was trained to hear.
